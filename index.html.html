About Me
The person behind the roadmap.

Hi, I'm Josh — a Senior Product Manager with a track record of building scalable platforms and data-driven products across SaaS, edtech, and cleantech. I've spent the last decade turning messy, ambiguous problems into shipped solutions that move real metrics.

My work spans everything from defining capability planning frameworks and data infrastructure strategy to launching client-facing tools using AI-assisted rapid prototyping. I've consistently secured executive alignment, restructured delivery teams, and shipped products ahead of schedule.

Before moving into product, I spent years in customer success and digital marketing — which means I genuinely understand what clients need and how to translate that into product decisions that drive both retention and revenue.

I hold a BA from Penn State and have worked with clients ranging from Expedia and Zillow to 400+ university systems across the country.
