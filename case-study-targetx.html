Background

A tool that wasn't keeping up with advisors' needs

When I joined TargetX, the Student Success Center dashboard was already behind. Advisors were working in a fragmented environment, their primary tool showed them a basic view of tasks and appointments, but gave them little meaningful context about the students they were responsible for.

The platform flagged students without explaining why. It didn't connect data from other key systems. And it offered no way to identify trends or proactively intervene before problems became crises. Advisors were reactive by necessity, not by choice.

The Problem

What the old experience looked like

The original dashboard gave advisors a high-level snapshot, tasks, a student count, and appointments for the day. It was functional, but it was essentially a to-do list with a student tally attached.

Before
The original dashboard, high-level task summary with limited student context

The student list view provided slightly more granularity, but was still missing the connective tissue advisors needed. Success scores existed but weren't actionable, there was no way to understand what was driving a low score, or how to prioritize outreach across a caseload.

Before
The student list, scores visible, but limited context for prioritization or action
  • ⚠️ Students were flagged as "at risk" without explanatory context. Advisors didn't know where to start
  • ⚠️ No integration with other key data systems. Advisors had to switch between tools to get a full picture
  • ⚠️ No trend visibility. The dashboard only showed today, not patterns over time
  • ⚠️ High-volume caseloads meant advisors couldn't give attention where it mattered most
  • ⚠️ Reactive by design. The tool had no mechanism for proactive intervention
The Solution

A force multiplier for advisors and their institutions

The new dashboard brought data from across the TargetX ecosystem into a single, actionable platform. Advisors could now see not just who needed help today, but identify patterns and trends that would have long-term impact on retention, enrollment, and revenue for their schools.

We modernized the information architecture, redesigned the data visualization layer, and built intuitive drill-down capabilities, so advisors could move from "something's wrong" to "here's exactly what I'm going to do" without leaving the platform.

After
The redesigned dashboard, real-time risk segmentation, trend tracking, and a fully integrated task and appointments view
  • ✅ Students segmented by risk level with clear, data-driven context. Advisors know who to call first and why
  • ✅ Integrated data from multiple systems into one unified view. No more tab switching
  • ✅ Weekly trend charts showing open issues over time. Proactive advising by default, not reactive
  • ✅ "My Students / All Students" toggle for caseload management at any scale
  • ✅ Appointments, tasks, and alerts surfaced in a single workflow. Reduced cognitive load across the board
Outcomes

Results that moved the needle

The new platform drove measurable engagement and satisfaction improvements across the client base. Advisors adopted the new features quickly because the experience was intuitive and the value was immediate, and schools saw downstream benefits in student retention and advisor capacity.
